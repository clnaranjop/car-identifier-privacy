Privacy Policy for Car Identifier

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

Introduction

Car Identifier ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Developer: Digitizing Collections
Contact Email: digitizingcollections@gmail.com

Information We Collect

1. Camera Permissions

  • What we collect: The App requires camera access to capture photos of die-cast model cars for identification purposes.
  • How we use it: Images are processed locally on your device using AI/machine learning models. We do NOT upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers.
  • Storage: Photos are temporarily stored on your device only during the identification process and can be deleted by you at any time.

2. Collection Data

  • What we collect: Information about die-cast cars you save to your personal collection (model name, year, series, date added).
  • How we use it: This data is stored locally on your device to provide collection management features.
  • Storage: All collection data remains on your device. We do not have access to this information.

3. Advertising Data (AdMob)

  • Third-party service: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App.
  • Data collected by AdMob: Google AdMob may collect certain information including:
    • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
    • IP address
    • Device information and specifications
    • Usage data and ad interaction data
  • Purpose: To serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Network Access

  • Purpose: Required for:
    • Loading advertisements (AdMob)
    • Checking for app updates
  • What we DON'T do: We do not transmit your photos, collection data, or personal information to our servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number
  • We do NOT upload or store your photos on external servers
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your contacts, messages, or other apps
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties

How We Use Information

  1. Camera Images: Processed locally using on-device AI models for car identification
  2. Collection Data: Stored locally to provide collection management features
  3. Advertising: AdMob uses device information to serve relevant ads

Data Storage and Security

  • All user data (photos, collection) is stored locally on your device
  • No user data is transmitted to our servers
  • You can delete all app data at any time through your device settings or the App's "Reset Collection" feature
  • Third-party services (AdMob) follow their own security protocols
